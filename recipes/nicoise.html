Salade nicoise

Fomous french salade

Salade niçoise (French pronunciation: [saˈlad niˈswaz]), salada nissarda in the Niçard dialect of the Occitan language, insalata nizzarda in Italian, is a salad that originated in the French city of Nice. It is traditionally made of tomatoes, hard-boiled eggs, Niçoise olives and anchovies or tuna, dressed with olive oil, or in some historical versions, a vinaigrette.

Ingridients

  • Tuna
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Green beans
  • Olive oil
  • 5 potatoes
  • Black olives
  • Lettuc leaves

Steps

  1. Place lettuce leaves on a plate.
  2. Peel the potatoes boil and cut into quarters.
  3. Cut tomatoes and eggs into slices.
  4. Add olives.
  5. Add tuna on top.
  6. Add green beans.
  7. Make a sause with olive oil and a little lemon juice.
  8. A little French spices.
  9. A little salt
  10. Salad is ready
