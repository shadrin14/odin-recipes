French crepes

A crêpe or crepe is a very thin type of pancake. Crêpes are usually one of two varieties: sweet crêpes (crêpes sucrées) or savoury galettes (crêpes salées). They are often served with a wide variety of fillings such as cheese, fruit, vegetables, meats, and a variety of spreads.[4] Crêpes can also be flambéed, such as in crêpes Suzette.
