Galushka's

This simple dish has its own monument in Poltava

Ingridients

  • cottage cheese 250g
  • Egg 60g
  • Wheat flour 50g
  • Korn flour 100g
  • Sour cream 50g
  • Sugar to tasty

Recipe

  1. Put in a deep bowl of cottage cheese, chicken egg, wheat and corn flour, sour cream and sugar to taste.
  2. Mix your dough carefully until smooth.
  3. Form small round galushki from the test.
  4. Carefully place galushki in boiling water.
  5. Break the gallushki before the moment they do not pop up. After cooking gallushki until ready for another 4-5 minutes.
  6. Drain the water into colander
  7. Spread dumplings on portions, add creamy oil and serve to the table.
