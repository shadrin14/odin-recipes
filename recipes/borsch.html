Borsch

One of the most famous Ukrainian dishes.

Ingridients

  • Chicken meat (pork or beef) 700g
  • Onion 100g
  • Carrot 100g
  • Beetroot 200g
  • Potato 200g
  • Cabbage 250g
  • Red beans
  • Garlic to taste
  • Tomato paste 100g
  • Water 5l
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

  1. Add the meat to the water and simmer until the meat is tender
  2. While the meat is cooking, peel the vegetables
  3. chop the carrots and onions, cut the beets and cabbage into strips
  4. Cut the potatoes into cubes
  5. Fry chopped beets in oil along with tomato paste
  6. add a little sugar and lemon juice
  7. When the meat is ready,you need to gently remove it from the broth and cool while debugging
  8. Add cabbage, potatoes, onions with carrots, a package of red beans, fried beets with tomato paste, a little garlic to the broth it turn and the meat is fiberized
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  10. Cook a little more until vegetables are ready, serve with sour cream and chopped dill
  11. Bon appetit!
